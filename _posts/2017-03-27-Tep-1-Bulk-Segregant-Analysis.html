--- layout: no-title-post title: "Tep-1 Bulk Segregant Analysis" date: 2017-03-27 ---

Tep-1 is an S. lycopersicum shade avoidance mutant identified by Leslie Herrera during her time with Julin Maloof. Julin asked me to continue her analysis when I approached him about an internship position in his lab. The analysis involved remapping Heinz reads from a prior experiment against the S. lycopersicum 2.50 genome in an attempt to clean up the VCF files Leslie had already generated.
